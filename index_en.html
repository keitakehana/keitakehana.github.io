Keisuke Takehana    Ph.D. (Engineering)

Research Fellow, Space Robotics Lab (Prof. Yoshida's Lab), Department of Aerospace Engineering, Tohoku University

Planetary Robotics Field Robotics Terramechanics
Keisuke Takehana is a Research Fellow in Space Robotics Laboratory at Tohoku University. His research interests include planetary exploration robots and uneven terrain mobile robots. His main research area is terramechanics. He conducts experimental research that contributes to the development of wheeled locomotion mechanisms, wheel design, performance evaluation, and driving control.
